Important Notes:

  1. All Attachments are the fully upgraded versions

  2. Some guns should probably be nerfed

Pistol

  • The Pistol is mainly used as a starter weapon and should only be used as a last resort as the Pistol doesn't do that much damage.

  • The Pistol has a charged energy shot that deals slightly more damage.

Combat Shotgun

  • The Standard Version shoots one round per click

The Combat Shotgun has 2 attachments

  • The Explosive shot which explodes upon impact

  • The Charged Burst fires multiple rounds in a matter of seconds

Super Shotgun

  • A sawed off double barrel shotgun that shoots 2 rounds at once dealing huge amounts of damage

Heavy Assault Rifle

  • The Standard Version shoots one round per click

The Heavy Assault Rifle has 2 attachments

  • The Scoped Version has a regular scope for better accuracy across long distances

  • The Micro Missles charges up and shoots up to 12 rounds at once

Plasma Rifle

  • The Standard Version shoots medium sized blue orbs

The Plasma Rifle has 2 attachments

  • The Heat Blast charges up as you fire upon demons and releases a large wave that kills surrounding enemies

  • The Stun Bomb charges up like the Heat blast but stuns an enemy

Chaingun

  • The Standard Version is a minigun that overheats

The Chaingun has 2 Attachments

  • The Gatling Rotator which never overheats

  • The Mobile Turret is a dual minigun that is stationary and overheats

Gauss Cannon

  • The Standard Version is a high powered beam that deals high damage

The Gauss Cannon has 2 attachments

  1. The Precision Bolt is like that of the Heavy Assault Rifle but deals slightly more damage than the Standard Version

  2. The Siege Mode forces the player to be stationary as it aims but deals exponentially more damage than the Precision Bolt

Rocket Launcher

  • The Rocket Launcher shoots rockets

The Rocket Launcher has 2 Attachments

  1. The Remote Detonation allows the player to detonate the missles at any moment after fired

  2. The Lock-On Burst locks on to an enemy and fires multiple rounds at once

Chainsaw

  • Slices through enemies like cold butter

  • Drops ammunition upon killing an enemy

BFG-9000

  • The most OVERPOWERED gun in the game

  • When fired, the gun releases a green orb round that insta kills most enemies and deals more damage the farther it gets

  • If used correctly, the BFG-9000 can kill a Baron of Hell
