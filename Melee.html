Fists

  • Like most FPS video games, the main character has fists. It's no different in this game as the first 3 kills by Doomguy are with his fists.

  • Now, I don't recommend you using fists as they do 1 SINGULAR hit point of damage.

  • The ONLY time when you should use the melee attack is to Glory Kill a Staggered enemy
